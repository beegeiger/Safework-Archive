Contact Us!

This site is brand new and there are a number of features still in progress. In the near future, you'll be able to create your own discussion forums, but until then, e-mail safeworkapp@gmail.com to give suggestions and we'll also create forums for any topics that gain popularity in the main forums.
The map only has strong data from the Bay Area. Some cities don't in any way publish their data, but some do. If you work in a city and would like for that city to be covered in the sex work arrest map, again e-mail us at safeworkapp@gmail.com and your city will be put at the top of our long list of cities to add!
If you have any other comments or questions on how we can improve the site, let us know! If you are a developer and would like to contribute, e-mail us, we can discuss what's needed, and we'll link you to the git!
The next big feature to be added (hopefully by fall 2018) is a feature where workers can customize automatic safety alerts. It will be able to be set up so once a worker goes out on the/a job, it can require periodic check-ins online, through e-mail or over SMS. After some period of not checking in, the app will automatically text and then call the worker to elicit a reponse (potentially a pre-set pin number or message). After a preset time, if the app still hasn't received a notice of safety from the worker, it will automatically e-mail, SMS, and/or call preset friends or family and provide them with the details and timeline of events. It will be an optional setting, but the app will be able to track the worker's phone's GPS and hopefully provide their people with their exact location.
E-mail safeworkapp@gmail.com with any comments!
